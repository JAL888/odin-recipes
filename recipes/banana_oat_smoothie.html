Banana Oat Smoothie

Description

Get a healthy start with lean protein from milk and yogurt, plus fiber from oats and banana.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup rolled oats (50 g)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk (240 mL), of choice

Methodology

  1. Add rolled oats to a blender and blend until the oats are the size of a fine crumb.
  2. Add the banana and milk and blend well.
  3. Pour in a glass.
  4. Enjoy!
